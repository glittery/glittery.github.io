Chocolate Cake Cookies

1 package chocolate cake mix 2 eggs 1tbsp water ½ cup shortening Bake at 375F for 8-10 min.

Kahlua Cake

1 package yellow cake mix 1 cup oil ¾ cup kahlua 1 package instant chocolate pudding 4 eggs ¾ cup water mix all ingredients above. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 min. cool in pan for 30 min. for icing ½ cup powdered sugar ½ cup kahlua

Corn Cassarole

[1/2 cup butter 2 egg whites 1 package cornbread mix 1 can whole corn (drained) 1 can creamed corn 1 cup sour cream Double recipe (but use 5 egg whites) Bake in 9x13 tray 55 minutes to 1 hour 15 minutes at 350°]

Grandma's Gingerbread Cookies

[Mix: 1/3 cup shortening 1 cup packed brown sugar 1-1/2 cups black molasses (12 oz jar Grandma molasses) Stir in 1/2 cup cold water Add: 7 cups flower 1 tsp salt 1 tsp allspice 1 tsp ground ginger 1 tsp ground gloves 1 tsp cinnamon Add: 2 tsp baking soda dissolved in 3 tbsp cold water Mix above ingredients thoroughly. Chill the dough overnight or longer. Roll out dough on floured board about 3/8" thick. Bake 350° on teflon baking sheets, 12-15 min. Do not overbake Cool first, then decorate. ]
