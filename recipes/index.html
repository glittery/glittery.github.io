Grandma's Gingerbread Cookies

[Mix:
1/3 cup shortening
1 cup packed brown sugar
1-1/2 cups black molasses (12 oz jar Grandma molasses)

Stir in 1/2 cup cold water

Add:

7 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground gloves
1 tsp cinnamon

Add: 2 tsp baking soda dissolved in 3 tbsp cold water

Mix above ingredients thoroughly.
Chill the dough overnight or longer.
Roll out dough on floured board about 3/8" thick.
Bake 350° on teflon baking sheets, 12-15 min. Do not overbake
Cool first, then decorate. ]
